# ClassicFitness Gym Website

Welcome to ClassicFitness, a simple frontend gym website created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Features

- Gym Programs: Explore our diverse range of workout programs designed to suit different fitness goals.
- Fees: Check out our membership fees and choose the plan that fits your needs.
- Trainers: Meet our experienced and certified trainers dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals.
- Schedule: View the gym schedule to plan your workout sessions.
- BMI Calculator: Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) to assess your fitness level.
